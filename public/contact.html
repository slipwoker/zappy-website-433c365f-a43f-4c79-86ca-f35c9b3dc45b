Common Questions

Quick answers to help you understand our platform capabilities and deployment process.

What data sources does Sentinel Intelligence support?

Our platform integrates AIS vessel tracking, ADS-B aircraft feeds, satellite telemetry, and proprietary maritime intelligence streams. We support both real-time and historical data ingestion with automatic normalization across sources.

What deployment options are available?

We offer cloud-hosted SaaS, private cloud deployment, and on-premises installation for air-gapped environments. All deployment models include the same core visualization and analysis capabilities with configurable data retention policies.

How does blackout detection work?

Our algorithms continuously monitor expected signal patterns and flag anomalies when vessels or aircraft go silent in monitored zones. The system correlates transponder outages with geofencing rules and historical behavior to minimize false positives.

What is the typical onboarding timeline?

Initial setup and training typically complete within 2-4 weeks, depending on deployment complexity and data source integration requirements. Enterprise deployments with custom workflows may require additional configuration time.

How do replay and auditing features work?

Every tracked movement is timestamped and stored for historical playback. You can scrub through past events, overlay multiple timeframes, export trajectory data, and generate audit reports with full chain-of-custody documentation for compliance purposes.

What does a demo session include?

Our demo covers live data visualization, closed airspace monitoring, blackout detection workflows, trajectory analysis tools, and custom alerting configuration. We tailor each session to your specific use case and operational requirements.
